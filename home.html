What We Do

NICU Solutions is dedicated to promoting excellence in the care of premature and critically ill newborns. Combining more than 60 years of Neonatal Intensive Care experience, we provide the utmost in neonatal healthcare expertise. Services include on-line and continuing education programs as well as professional consultation related to neonatal intensive care. Our goal is to improve the quality of neonatal healthcare, and to present opportunities for professional growth for all neonatal healthcare providers.

Our internationally-recognized Comprehensive Neonatal Review and NICU Certification Preparation Program has been attended by more than 4000 participants from across the United States and Canada, with a greater than 95% success rate on the national certification exam for neonatal nurses, based on combined self-reports and reports by participating institutions.

We also provide a wide variety of educational lectures and programs, which are easily customized to fit your needs. Check out our NICU Lecture Series page for a list of topics. Don't see what you're looking for? Contact us about customizing a program just for you!
