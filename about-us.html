About Us

Linda J. Juretschke, PhD, RNC-NPT, APN/NNP-BC, CCNS

Linda is the Founder and President of NICU Solutions, Inc., and has been involved in neonatal nursing since 1983. She has worked in a variety of roles, including staff nurse, charge nurse, transport nurse, preceptor, clinical educator, neonatal nurse practitioner, and case manager. She has published widely in national journals including Neonatal Network, JOGNN, and The American Journal of Perinatology, and has lectured extensively across the United States on a variety of neonatal topics. Linda is involved in many professional organizations, and is named in the Sigma Theta Tau International Media Guide to Health Care Experts.


Contact Linda at linda@nicusolutions.com

Robert E. Juretschke, BHA, RRT, RCP

Bob is Co-founder and Vice President of NICU Solutions, Inc. He has been a Respiratory Care Practitioner since 1984. He has a wide variety of experience in all areas of respiratory care, and has functioned in the role of staff respiratory care practitioner, supervisor, and clinical educator. He has presented lectures on a variety of respiratory topics, and is involved in many professional organizations. Bob has been involved in neonatal care for more than 25 years.


Contact Bob at linda@nicusolutions.com
